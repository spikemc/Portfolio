Yarn

  • Platform: iOS

  • Status: Yarn shut down in 2016

Yarn was a start up that was a part of the Samsung Accelerator (now called Samsung NEXT) in New York City.

Contents

  • Challenge

  • Solution

  • Role

Challenge

How do you improve the way in which people interact with the thousands of photos in their Camera Rolls? How does the changing way we take photos impact the way that we remember? How do we leverage the camera roll to remember?

Solution

  • An app called Yarn. Yarn was a photo management app that would make finding your photos easy through metadata. Yarn encouraged users to enter information about their photos in order to make them more accessible, and then served up photo montages and memories based on specific criteria.

Role


  • Defined product: interviewed target audience, worked with CEO to define product roadmap, product functionality, write user stories.

  • Managed workflow: Worked with CEO and team to define product backlog, create user stories, groom backlog.

  • Managed implementation: worked with front/back end developers, and UI/visual designer to define and implement the Yarn user experience.

  • Managed users: invited test users, solicited feedback, implemented and reviewed analytics.
