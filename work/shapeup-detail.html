About

Shape Up Or Die is a time based online computer game developed by myself, Steve Klise, Meghan Hoke and Sebastian Buys for ITP's Big Games class.

Player's control small ships and attempt to score points by running over and "killing" other players. The ships are able to move as far as they want within the boundaries, but only receive a certain number of changes in direction. To play, users map out their moves, and set their ship lose. For every 8 minutes the player is "logged out", they receive an extra change of direction, up to 10. As the ships move around the board, pieces of terrain and useful items spawn.
