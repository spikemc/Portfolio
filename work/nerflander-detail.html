Nerflander

About

Nerflander was an interactive swordfighting game created by Jackson Snellings, Nara Kasbergen and myself for the final of our Introduction to Physical Computing Class in the Fall of 2010.

The object of the game was to incapacitate your opponent by scoring hits with a Nerf sword sheathed in copper tape on the areas of copper mesh attached to the sweatshirt. The hits were recorded by a Processing sketch that displayed the damage on Fallout inspired cartoon characters.
