About

"The Incident: Sweded" was created for ITP's annual "4 projects in 4 days" marathon.

The concept of "sweded" videos comes from Michel Gondry's 2008 film "Be Kind, Rewind". We decided to swede the Big Bucket iPhone game "The Incident". We cut, painted, set up, filmed and edited the entire piece in a single day.

"The Incident: Sweded" was created by Greg Borenstein, Mike Cohen, Steve Klise, David Phillips, Liza Singer and myself.
