How Are You

About

"How Are You" is a piece I created for my Data Representation class taught by Jer Thorpe.

The box contains a small solenoid connected to an Arduino. The solenoid fires every time the current time matches the time a chat message was sent or received by me on a certain day (for this demonstration I was using a day back in May of 2009), hitting the side of the box and creating a "thok" sound. "How Are You" was created using a combination of Python, Processing and Arduino.

The piece is meant to demonstrate the interruption that chats play in my life by magnifying and sonically representing the impact on my attention span every time I receive a chat.
