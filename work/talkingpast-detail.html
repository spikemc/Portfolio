Talking to your past

About

Talking to Your Past is a project I made for Art of the Archive, a class taught at ITP in the Fall of 2011 by Michael Connor.

Talking to Your Past attempts to provoke self-reflection and critical analysis by exposing users to past online conversations in real time. Talking to Your Past was created specifically for Gmail, and allows the user to log in, search, and re-experience conversations stored in their Gmail chat archive.

As we rely increasingly on digital and text-based interactions we have an ever accumulating record of our day to day lives. Talking to Your Past attempts to interrogate what this record means and what it can teach us about our past.
