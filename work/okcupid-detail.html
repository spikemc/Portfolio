OkCupid?

About

OkCupid is a video piece that explores the way that people present themselves on the internet. The explosion of social-networks and online dating has created an environment where users must compete with others to attract attention and potential mates. This leads to a dialogue that is increasingly divorced from reality.

OkCupid attempts to expose this disconnect through decontextualization. By vocalizing these profiles, I hope to highlight the strange ways in which it has become acceptable to try and lure in potential mates.
